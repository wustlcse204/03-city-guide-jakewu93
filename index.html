A guide to some best things to do in L.A.

LOS ANGELES

L.A. is the largest city in the U.S. state of California. With the population of around 3.9 million in 2020, it is the second largest city in the U.S. to New York City. Los Angeles has a diverse economy, and hosts businesses in a broad range of professional and cultural fields. It also has the busiest container port in the Americas. In 2018, the Los Angeles metropolitan area had a gross metropolitan product of over $1.0 trillion,making it the city with the third-largest GDP in the world, after Tokyo and New York City.

5 Best Places to Visit in L.A.


1. Never Miss the View of Downtown L.A.

From various beautiful buildings and the attractive skyline, downtown L.A. makes itself a must-go place!


2. Enjoy the Best Summer Beach Vacation in Santa Monica

Summer, beach, ocean, sunset, roller coaster, ferris wheels and so on, you will have the most romantic memory in Santa Monica!


3. Get in Touch with Cosmos at Griffith Observatory

Through the Hall of Sky and the Hall of Eye, you will get closer touch with the cosmos at Griffith Observatory! Plus the overlook of the whole city from here will definitely surprise you!


4. Feel Like a Star at Hollywood

No one doesn't know about movies and arts at Hollywood! Want to feel like a movie star or get close to the Hollywood Sign? Go check Star Walk of Fame or for a hike on the hill!


5. IN-N-OUT Burger is a Must Try!

The local most famous burger restaurant that you cannot miss! Even though you may not like all the foods there, it is worth a try since you can't find it even most of the states of U.S.!
